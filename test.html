<b>Two Day Intensive Hands On Workshop </b>

<br><br>

Topics will be viewed from both the teachers' and the students' perspectives.

<br><br>

Session one focuses on:

<ul>

<li>Standard for Mathematical Practice 1: Make sense of problems and persevere in solving them</li>

<li> Modeling of effective teaching strategies</li>

<li> Writing in math class: descriptive, procedural and conceptual</li></ul>

For homework between sessions teacher will be asked to present tryouts to their algebra classes and be prepared to share the results of those tryouts in session two.<br><br>

Session two focuses on:<ul>

<li> Standard for Mathematical Practice 6: Attention to precision</li>

<li> Labeling in all aspects of student work</li>

<li> Communication: listening, speaking, writing</li>

<li> Raising the level of discourse in the classroom</li></ul><br>

Lunch provided

***Newcomers and participants from Workshops 1 & 2 in both March and October are invited to explore the Practices in the following workshops
